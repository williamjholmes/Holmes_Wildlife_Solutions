Our Services

Wildlife Management

Our experience shines in maintaining a healthy habitat, which can allow for strong genetics and trophy wildlife. This has been proven over the years with multiple entries for Whitetail Deer in the South Carolina Record Book, as well as success in local Wild Turkey competitions.

Feed Corn

As part of a new passion project, we now plan to plant, shell, and sell our own feed corn to the public.
Availability and prices may vary, contact us for their status.

Food Plots

Both the quality and quantity of your wildlife population begins with the food sources available. We offer many options for your food plots, including various Grains, Legumes, Brassicas, and cover crops. We can design the best plan for your food plots based on your goals, location, soil conditions, and other available food sources. Whether you hope to improve your results in Deer, Dove, Duck, or Turkey season, we can make it happen.
